Classic Hummus
Creamy, smooth, and ready in 10 minutes
1 שעה
הכנה
4
מנות
קל
רמה
מצרכים
400gcanned chickpeas, drained
3 tbsptahini
2 tbspfresh lemon juice
1 clovegarlic
2 tbspolive oil
½ tspsalt
3 tbspcold water
אופן הכנה
1
Add the chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice, and garlic to a food processor.
2
Blend for 1 minute, then slowly add cold water while blending until smooth and creamy.
3
Add olive oil and salt, blend for another 30 seconds. Taste and adjust.
4
Serve in a bowl, drizzle with olive oil, and sprinkle paprika on top.
Pro tip
For extra smooth hummus, peel the chickpeas before blending. It takes 5 extra minutes but makes a huge difference!
